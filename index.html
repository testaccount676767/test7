About AAB Bakery

Founded in 2019, AAB Bakery has become the sanctuary for artisan bread and pastry lovers in Singapore CBD. We combine traditional baking techniques with premium local ingredients to deliver products that elevate your daily indulgence.

Our team of passionate bakers cherish every step—from nurturing our sourdough starter to hand-decorating cakes—to ensure every bite is a memorable experience.
